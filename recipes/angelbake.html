Perfect chocolate cream dream angel bake

Description

This version of the Perfect Chocolate Dream Angel Bake incorporates some cream cheese to make a brownie/cheesecake hybrid. It is made by whipping egg whites to create lots of volume.

Ingredients

  • 9 tbsp (138 g) liquid egg whites ½ cup + 1 tbsp
  • ½ cup (113 g) unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 ½ scoops (45 g) vanilla casein protein powder
  • 1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla
  • 2 tsp (8 g) stevia
  • ¼ tsp (1 g) baking powder
  • 2 tbsp (30 g) mini chocolate chips
  • 3 tbsp (15 g) cocoa powder
  • ½ cup (113 g) plain non fat Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup (113 g) fat free cream cheese
  • 1 tsp (5 g) oat flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F
  2. Beat the egg whites, vanilla, & baking powder until stiff peaks form.
  3. Add ½ tsp stevia & cocoa powder.
  4. Add in applesauce & casein. You’ll lose a lot of the volume here.
  5. Pour ¾ of it into a greased 8×8. top with chocolate chips.
  6. Melt cream cheese in microwave. Stir in 1 ½ stevia, yogurt, and oat flour.
  7. Spread a layer of the cream cheese over the brownie. Add the remaining brownie to the top by the spoonful and marble with a knife.
  8. Bake for 20-23 minutes. Let cool before eating if you want it to be solid.
